# Dual Compliance Checklist: NDPA and GDPR

**Reference:** Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) | EU General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR)
**Applies To:** Businesses operating under both frameworks simultaneously
**Prepared by:** TrustGrid Technology

---

## How to Use This Checklist

This checklist is for businesses that must comply with both the NDPA and GDPR at the same time. Work through each section and mark your status honestly. Every gap you find here is a regulatory risk that needs a remediation plan.

**Status Key**
- ✅ Completed
- 🔄 In Progress
- ❌ Not Started
- N/A Not Applicable
